After an entire hour of hard work, the Penguin Launcher team is thrilled to announce our new program: **Penguin Premium!** This brings a new way to support the project, and give _you_ an even better Penguin Launcher experience.

## What is it?

Penguin Premium is an exciting new feature we're introducing in the upcoming Penguin 𝕏 (10.0), meant to help our community explore new opportunities with our launcher and how they play the game. We plan on constantly expanding this, with more and more features, cosmetics, and events being pushed out to the launcher on a regular basis. It will be all front and center going forward!

## How does it work?

We currently offer year-round benefits such as in-game cosmetics, access to more launcher features, and exclusive perks in our Discord server. Be on the look out for seasonable promotions as well!

Penguin Premium is separated into two tiers[:](https://i.imgur.com/KUEFPc2.png)

- **Gold ($5/month)**
  - 15 Minecraft instances (up from 5)
  - Limited ad experience
  - An exclusive role in our Discord server
  - Special in-game cape\*
  - 1GB cloud storage
  - Custom emoji anywhere
  - Access to in-game voice chat
- **Silver ($10/month)**
  - Unlimited Minecraft instances!
  - Ad-free experience
  - Get verified in-game\*
  - 2GB cloud storage
  - Customizable sprint effects for your player\*
  - Exclusive access to new cosmetics
  - Server support (TBD)

<sub><sup>\*</sup> Only visible to other Penguin Launcher users</sub>

## When can I sign up?!

Penguin Premium isn't quite ready yet, but we'll hopefully have some more news for you soon.
